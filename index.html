Piece of Poetries

O you drowsy,
Open your fiery eyes, and see the death of your certainty
The certainty you built on your broken voice, cheap tears.
All your failed devotions, hard works are questionable
All your ugly thoughts are burning in the catastrophe.

O you drowsy,
Put up your edifices, skyscrapers
none of has foundations, destroyed in the microseism.
The Consequences of your impersonation became silent language
That has given you today drumbeat of misfortune.

O you drowsy,
You have been guilty for no reason, being punished for silent guilt
You stab from behind, everyone says regardlessly.
Today you are a traitor, but you did nothing
You hit yourself, but people say others cry..

Wake up from your slumber, wake up your faith
Your mind is inferior, and the seers are not great.

O You Drowsy

Why in the fog of this soft night
Tears fall on the body of the vine?
Why in the silence of this deep night
Wailing comes from the bone marrow?
Why the intensity of this groaning
Takes away hundreds of nights of sleep and dreams?
Why do these afflicted souls
continue to be drowned in the quicksand of self-screamming?
Why the brain crushed in loneliness
Loses the power of judgement?
Why the ears drowned in the wailing of loneliness
can't hear the pale call of the future?

Then hidden in the flowering paradise of present
poisonous memories of the past stings,
Rights interferes in the arena of melody,
Innumerable arrows rive through the chest.
In the toxic drug addiction,
In the hoary pollution of that poisonous scent,
In the intoxication of that inaudible voice,
for which the dignity of the sensitive flower eroded,
I will end her chastise by drinking that poison.

The Toxic Intoxication
